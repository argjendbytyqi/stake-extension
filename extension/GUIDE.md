## 1. Installation
Since this is a high-performance specialized tool, you must load it manually into your browser:

1.  **Download** and extract the `StakePeekBlitz.zip` file.
2.  Open your browser (Chrome or Brave) and navigate to: `chrome://extensions`
3.  Enable **"Developer mode"** (top right toggle).
4.  Click **"Load unpacked"** (top left).
5.  Select the `extension` folder from your extracted files.

## 2. Activation
1.  Click the **StakePeek** icon in your browser toolbar (pin it for easy access).
2.  Paste your unique **License Key** into the input field.
3.  Click **"Activate System"**. 
4.  The status should change to **ACTIVE**.
